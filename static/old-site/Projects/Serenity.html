Serenity

April 6-7th, 2024



During my first Hackathon at Northwestern's Wildhacks, my team and I collaborated on a mental wellness application. The app lets users chat with others about their wellness to the message board. While the app doesn't do much, it was a valuable learning experience to work with others and produce something comprehensive.

Matt worked with the backend Firebase to allow users to login and comment to a database.
Leann designed the outline of the website, where things should be and how a user will navigate the website.
Isaiah and I worked with HTML and CSS to make the website elements work with each other.
I also starred in, directed, and edited the video you see above.
