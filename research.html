My research asks whether language models can write formal specifications that a model checker will actually accept. I work in the AI4FM / FMitF group at Loyola University Chicago under Prof. Konstantin Läufer.

Research Statement

Formal verification has guarded industrial systems at Amazon and Microsoft for years, yet writing a correct specification still takes an expert and a lot of time. If a language model could turn an English requirement into a spec that passes the checker, verification stops being a luxury. My work measures exactly how far today's models are from that — and tries to close the gap.
