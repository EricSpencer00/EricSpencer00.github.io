Anagram Solver in Java

Due to my love for Scrabble, I created an Anagram solver for the game.
Out of the 7 letters you have in your deck, the solver will tell you
all the possible words you can play, according to the official
scrabble dictionary.

It is coded entirely in Java, employing HashMaps and HashSets for
fast lookup based on your letters. In Scrabble, there are space
characters that allow for any letter to be played. This adds
complexity due to the amount of permutations possible for each of 26
letters to be compared with your stack.
