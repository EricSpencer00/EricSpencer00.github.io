### 📌 Features:
- 🔔 **Task notifications** to remind yourself
- 🏥 **Sort tasks** based on urgency (e.g., **Medicine vs. Quality of Life**)
- 🎉 **Confetti effect** when all tasks are completed
- 😁 **Custom emojis** for a personalized experience
