This project is a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) bot designed to assist the Loyola University Chicago's Information Technology Services (ITS) department. The bot can answer common questions from students and faculty, drawing from a knowledge base of ITS documentation.

The project is open source and available on GitHub: [https://github.com/EricSpencer00/ITS-RAG-bot](https://github.com/EricSpencer00/ITS-RAG-bot)
