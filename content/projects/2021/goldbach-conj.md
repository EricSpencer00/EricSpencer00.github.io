This project contains code to explore and test the Goldbach Conjecture, which states that every even integer greater than 2 is the sum of two prime numbers.

The repository is available on GitHub: [https://github.com/EricSpencer00/goldbach-conj](https://github.com/EricSpencer00/goldbach-conj)
